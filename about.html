Growth Equity

We invest in great businesses with strong growth and compelling valuations, as well as unique special situations. With a 3–5 year time horizon and a concentrated portfolio, we seek scalable opportunities in U.S.-listed equities across technology, media, and consumer sectors. Our process focuses on sustainable earnings growth and attractive entry points supported by a long-term investment thesis.

Sector Innovation

Our philosophy centers on identifying superior businesses—those with large addressable markets, market leadership, high returns on capital, capable management, reinvestment opportunities, and financial strength. We aim to own companies that can double earnings within 3–5 years and whose valuations underestimate future earnings power.

Global Expansion

We pursue opportunities where our research identifies underappreciated upside. Using data screening tools and proprietary models, we evaluate financial strength, growth potential, and valuation to inform our investment decisions. Our framework emphasizes clarity of thesis, resilience to macro shocks, and continuous evaluation based on fundamentals and market structure.

Our Business Principles

Our business is built on intellectual honesty, discipline, and alignment. We value transparency, a deep understanding of our investments, and thoughtful allocation based on conviction and risk. We prioritize owning businesses we understand and where we can clearly articulate why we own them—and why they will endure.

Steve Weinstein is the founder and managing partner of Ochoco Capital Management, LLC. With over 25 years of experience investing in high-growth technology and consumer businesses, Mr. Weinstein has been recognized as an Institutional Investor Allstar and Wall Street Journal Best on the Street. He held leadership roles at M Science and Pacific Crest Securities and worked on several high-profile IPOs including Alibaba, Mercado Libre, and Zillow. Based in Powell Butte, Oregon, Mr. Weinstein enjoys hiking, paddle boarding, and skiing.
